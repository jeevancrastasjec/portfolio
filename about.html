About

I'm Jeevan

Product designer and developer from Kerala, India.

Just a little bit about me- I'm a budding product designer who loves creating designs that are super user-friendly and look great.‍‍‍I got into design back in Grade 11, spending my free time making UIs using Adobe XD and getting inspiration from Pinterest. I also jumped into coding while learning about app development.‍

‍In 2020, when the COVID-19 pandemic hit and everyone was confined to their homes, I seized the opportunity to enhance my design and coding skills. I watched numerous YouTube videos to learn about design systems and user research. Simultaneously, I began creating complete Flutter apps and even published some on the Play Store.‍
